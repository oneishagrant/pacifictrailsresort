About Us

Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort, Where tranquility meets luxury on California's stunning north coast. Our resort offers a serene escape in nature, nestled between towering redwoods and the endless expanse of the Pacific Ocean. Each of our private yurts provides a unique blend of rustic charm and modern comfort, designed to help you unwind and reconnect with nature.

At Pacific Trails, we believe in creating unforgettable experiences. From our gourmet, locally sourced breakfasts to guided coastal tours, every detail is crafted with your relaxation in mind. Whether you're enjoying a quiet moment on our hiking trail, dining under the stars or exploring the rugged coastline, our resort is dedicated to providing you with an unparalleled retreat.

Join us at Pacific Trails Resort and discover the perfect balance of adventure and relaxation. We invite you to experience the beauty of the North Coast in a way that only Pacific Trails Resort can offer.
